Our Mission

At CDM, our mission is to spread the love and teachings of Jesus Christ to all people. We strive to live out His commandments and share His message of salvation.

02

Transformative Worship

Experience the power of worship that lifts our spirits, connects us with God, and transforms lives. Our services are filled with heartfelt praise, prayer, and the study of God's Word.
